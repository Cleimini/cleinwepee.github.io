ANIREPO

D I G I T A L A N I M E R E P O S I T O R Y

http://anirepo.epizy.com/

AniRepo is a website for you to keep track of your daily anime activities. Such as anime that you've finished watching, anime that you are currently watching, anime that you postponed watching, and anime that you plan to watch soon.

Another feature is adding anime to your favorites along with anime characters and voice actors/actresses. And anime that you reviewed/share your opinion.

Lastly, it also helps you to keep track of the current trending anime on the MyAnimeList website. Such as trending anime this season, newly updated episodes of a trending anime, trending airing anime, trending upcoming anime, and all-time popular anime.

All data whether it be anime, character or voice actor/actress are being scraped from the MyAnimeList website using a PHP library called Simple HTML DOM Parser.

Recent updates with the HTML structure of the MyAnimeList website prevents users to scrape data from it. As of the moment, I am currently working on finding another way of scraping the data without the help of Web Scraping APIs and Libraries. And to solely rely on the Simple HTML DOM Parser just for fun.

The PHP script above is the code in adding anime to your repository. By just providing the URL of an anime from the MyAnimeList website. The script will scrape the selected data from the website such as the:

  • Title

  • Type (TV, Movie, etc.)

  • Source (Manga, Visual Novel, etc.)

  • Premiered (Winter 2021, Summer 2020, etc.)

  • Studios (Studio Ghibli, Kyoto Animation, etc.)

  • Genres (Action, Adventure, etc.)

  • Thumbnail
