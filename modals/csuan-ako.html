PROJECT #1

CSUAN AKO: AN ONLINE ALUMNI REPOSITORY

http://alumni.cics-csuaparri.org.ph/

An alumni website dedicated to the Cagayan State University of Aparri. It features Alumni Directory, Alumni Tracer and Decision Support System.

The Alumni Directory is a feature on the website that allows users to search for an alumni member of the university ranging from 1960 - present year.

The Alumni Tracer accompanied with a Decision Support System is a feature on the website that determines the employment status of its graduates from various programs offered by the institutions. Thiswill also served as a tool to update you about the events, milestones and activities of the Institutions.
