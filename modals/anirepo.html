PROJECT #2

ANIREPO: DIGITAL ANIME REPOSITORY

http://anirepo.epizy.com/

An anime website designed to store your daily anime activities digitally. It saves all of the anime that you've Finished Watching, anime that you are Currently Watching, anime that you Postponed Watching, and anime that you Plan to Watch.

Additional features also includes saving your Favorite Anime, Favorite Character, Favorite Voice Actor/Actress. Along with Finished Anime that you Reviewed.

Data gathering from each anime, character or voice actor/actress is done via Web Scraping using the Simple HTML DOM Parser. Its function primarily scrapes the needed data from the MyAnimeList website. Datas scraped from an anime for example are the following:

  1. Anime Title
  2. Anime Type (TV, Movie OVA, ONA, Special)
  3. Premiered (Spring, Summer, Fall, and Winter of all Years)
  4. Studios (Ghibli Studio, Kyoto Animation, ufotable, etc.)
  5. Source (Manga, Visual Novel, Game, etc.)
  6. Genres (Action, Adventure, Fantasy, etc.)
  7. Thumbnails

Besides scraping data for the anime, characters, and voice actors/actresses. The website also scrapes data from the targeted to Trending Anime this Season, Newly Updated Episodes of Trending Anime, Trending Top Anime, Trending Upcoming Anime, and All-time Popular Anime.
